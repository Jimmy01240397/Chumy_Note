Create:
fdisk /dev/sd[b-f]1 => 
	n (1)
	t (29 = Linux Raid)
	w

mdadm -E /dev/sd[b-f]1
mdadm --create /dev/md0 --level=6 --raid-devices=5 /dev/sd[b-f]1


Add disk:
mdadm --add /dev/md0 /dev/sdf1


Resize:
mdadm --grow --size=max /dev/md0
resize2fs -p /dev/md0


Remove:
mdadm --stop /dev/md0
mdadm --zero-superblock /dev/sd[b-f]1
